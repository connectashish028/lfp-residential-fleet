Pipeline contract

Every script is idempotent. Re-running any one step rebuilds its output from the previous layer — never partial state. The parquets are the contract; the DuckDB catalogue is expendable.

Single source of truth

Streamlit pages never query DuckDB directly and never do math. They call data.compute_X() only. That's why the ID17 DoD-vs-RTE slope reads identically on the Overview callout, the System status pill, and the Peer Comparison chart title.
